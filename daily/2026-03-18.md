## Competitor Monitor — Concept Breakdown

The Idea of the Day was sharp enough to document properly. The concept: an agent that crawls competitor websites and social channels on a weekly cadence, extracts signals (new products, pricing shifts, campaign themes), and delivers a structured briefing to Telegram every Monday.

**Architecture is straightforward:**
- `tavily_crawl` handles the web traversal — no scraper to maintain
- Claude summarises and classifies signals (product / pricing / marketing)
- OpenClaw cron schedules the weekly run and handles Telegram delivery
- Config lives in a JSON file: competitor URLs, signal categories, delivery channel

**Why it matters for HD India:** the window coverings market is competitive but under-monitored. Luxaflex, Sunwood, and a handful of regional players all have web presence. Siva currently tracks this manually (or not at all). An automated weekly brief costs < 1 day to build and delivers real commercial intelligence. Effort: 2. Impact: 4. That ratio is hard to argue with.
