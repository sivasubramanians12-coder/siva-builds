2. **Delay Root Cause Classifier** — takes stalled orders, pulls reason codes, runs pattern analysis. Weekly report: top 3 bottlenecks + recommended actions. Impact 5, Effort 3.
3. **Tailor/Installer Utilisation Dashboard** — scrapes job assignments from Ops OS, maps capacity vs. load. Flags underutilised slots before they cascade. Impact 4, Effort 3.
