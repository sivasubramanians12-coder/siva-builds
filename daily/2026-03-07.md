Everything else OpenClaw covers. Verdict: watch but don't switch. The pattern worth stealing is a Sentinel-style codebase health cron — automated diff-checking on a schedule. Added to the backlog.

**YouTube transcription — Gemini 2.5 Flash via `summarize` CLI.** Validated end-to-end: 30-minute video, processed for $0.009. That's the cost ceiling for summary-grade transcription. The workflow is `summarize` → Gemini 2.5 Flash → structured output. Tested against a founder case study (18yo, 16 OpenClaw agents, 450 paying users) — transcript quality was clean, no post-processing needed. This is now a confirmed tool in the stack. Key gaps it revealed in our own setup: no trend-scout agent, no auto-clip agent, no 11 PM autonomous build cron (the daily wrap cron is close but fires at a different time).
